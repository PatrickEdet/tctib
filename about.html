Welcome to Teachers Continuous Training Institute

Our

VISION

The vision of the Institute is to be a World Class academic environment that will support and sustain the continuous re-training of Teachers who will champion the highest quality of teaching and learning in Primary and Secondary Schools, as well as lift them to the highest performance in all examinations.

Our

MISSION

The mission of the Institute is to create an Academic atmosphere that will promote high quality teaching and learning to keep teachers up to date with the teaching needs of schools, to attract high quality entrants into the teaching profession and produce learners that can excel in all internal and external assessment as part of Teacher development.

Our Objectives

Equipping serving teachers at all levels with necessary up-to-date pedagogical skill, knowledge, and the use of instructional materials in classroom instrucion and management.

Faciitating the development of positive self-concept and attitude towards the teaching profression.

Providing courses of instruction for teaher development and making those facilities available to persons and organisations that need to benefit from them.

Collaborating with Organisations, Institutes and Bodies with similar Objectives, for the improvement of the knowledge ecosystem.

Undertaking other activities appropriate for an institution of the highest sandard.

Carrying out other objectives for the fulfilment of the Vision and Mission of the Institute
