Welcome to Teachers Continuous Training Institute

Our

VISION

The vision of the Institute is to be a World Class academic environment that will support and sustain the continuous re-training of Teachers who will champion the highest quality of teaching and learning in Primary and Secondary Schools, as well as lift them to the highest performance in all examinations.

Our

MISSION

The mission of the Institute is to create an Academic atmosphere that will promote high quality teaching and learning to keep teachers up to date with the teaching needs of schools, to attract high quality entrants into the teaching profession and produce learners that can excel in all internal and external assessment as part of Teacher development.

About Teachers Continous Training Institute

Teachers Continuous Training Institute (TCTI) is one among the capital projects initiated and established by the Government of Cross River State under the administration of His Excellency Prof. Sir Ben Ayade to improve the wellbeing of 'Crossiverians' through job creation and to promote the standard of quality teachers in the country.

The Executive Governor of Cross River State, His Excellency Prof. Sir Ben Ayade has changed the socio-economic narrative of the people of Cross River State through the establishment of the Teachers Continuous Training Institute in Biase Local Government Area of Cross River State.

The Institute is aimed at providing an enabling environment for serving teachers at all levels including the primary, junior and senior secondary schools, at both private and public sectors to update their knowledge and skills in teaching pedagogies, in all areas/subjects that are taught in WAEC, NECO and Public Examinations.

The Institute has the facilities to provide qualitative training and retraining opportunities to serving teachers in Cross River State and all over Nigeria, including other parts of African Countries respectively. This vision is to ensure that teachers are abreast with the new methodologies of teaching that meets the digital age in the 21st Century.

However, it is often said that a teacher's reward is in heaven, this statement does not always go well with some persons, thereby distorting their ambition of being teachers and they do not value the profession because everybody wants to enjoy the dividend of his labour while on earth.

It was on this note that the Governor changed the scenario and established Teachers Continuous Training Institute so that teachers will get their reward in the Institute.
Teachers are now recognized as one of the top leaders all over the world. As Nigerians join the entire world on every October 5th to celebrate the World Teachers Day (WTD), the efficacy of this annual event would be useful as the Teachers Continuous Training Institute is the World Training Institute to provide teachers with the current pedagogical skills, knowledge and the use of methodology and instructional materials in the classroom to enhance teaching and learning.

TCTI is equipped with enormous and modern facilities that can hardly be found in other parts of African countries. Such facilities are now available for teachers to explore.

Geographical Location

TCTI is located at the Southern Senatorial District of Cross River State in Biase Local Government Area, 89km away from Calabar, the capital city of Cross River State. The Institute shares a common boundary with the Local Government Headquarter, Akpet Central.

Biase, Cross River State, Nigeria

Brief History of TCTI

Teachers Continuous Training Institute (TCTI) Biase, was established by the Government of Cross River State, under the leadership of the Executive Governor His Excellency, Senator Prof. Ben Ayade in the year 2020.

The Institute took-up fully on the 9th of September 2021 and is headed by the pioneer Director-General, Prof. Taoheed Adedoja and the Deputy Director- General, Mrs. Mary Omaji.
Other Principal Officers are the Registrar, Mrs. Rebecca Joseph Odumusor, the Bursar Mrs. Margaret Odey, Senior Librarian Dr. Emmanuel U. Omini, the Director of Academic and Professional Services Dr. Godwin Ogar, the Acting Director of Works Mr. Obase – T. O. Abang, the Principal of TCTI Demonstration Secondary School Mr. Fidelis Erenje, and the Vice Principal Mrs. Ibor Efayemodo Omini, Acting Chief Security Officer Rt. ASP Robert Agabi.

Facilities in TCTI

As at the time of this publication, the Institute was graced with modern facilities such as the fully equipped ICT Laboratory, Four Science Laboratories in areas of Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Agriculture, Library, a 1000 sitting capacity multipurpose hall with a stage and sound system, an Amphitheatre of 448 sitting capacity with sound system and lounge behind. The Institute also has a clinic to ensure adequate care and good health, a 100 suite room for participants/trainee teachers, an Executive Conference and Academic Boardroom, standard boreholes for adequate water reticulation, and a demonstration secondary school.

It also has a very beautiful cafeteria with best hygiene practices, and hotels for guests and students hostel. The Institute has a 270kva power supplying generator and a fence around the entire institute.

A well serene environment decorated with beautiful natural flowers, sports arena under construction and well tarred road network all over the vicinity.
