ABOUT THE SCHOOL

The Demonstration Secondary School of Teachers Continuous Training Institute, (TCTI) is located within the TCTI premises, which is adjacent to Biase Local Government Secretariat in Cross River State. It is well equipped with state of the art facilities for boarding. The Demonstration Secondary School,established in 2021, has highly qualified teachers, and is located in a very serene, conducive and safe environment for teaching and learning, and maintains high level of discipline for its students.
Vacancies exist for Junior Secondary and Senior Secondary Classes at the School.

Tuition is free for the Junior Secondary section, while Fees are charged per term for the Senior Secondary section for boarding facilities.

FEATURES AND SPECIAL ATTRACTIONS

  1. The Teachers in the school are all Qualified Graduates of Education that have met the Digital Literacy Standards for the profession.
  2. Well-Furnished Accommodation (Dormitories)
  3. Good Feeding with Balanced Diet for The Students
  4. Free Tuition Fees (For JSS1-3)
  5. Secure Environment and Proper Protection for The Students and Teachers
  6. Facilities are Available for Christian and Muslim Worship
  7. Well-Equipped and Comfortable Classrooms
  8. High Quality Facilities for Learning
  9. Internet Facilities Available
  10. Full Boarding
  11. Scholarship for One Year For Best Performed Candidate for the Senior Secondary School in the Entrance Examination
  12. Scholarship for The All-Round Best Student for the Senior Secondary School at the End of Each Academic Year
