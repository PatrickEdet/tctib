Demonstration Secondary School of Teachers Continous Training Institute Admission Form

GUIDELINES FOR SALE OF FORMS AND CONDUCT OF THE ENTRANCE EXAMINATION FOR THE 2022/23 ADMISSION OF STUDENTS FROM ALL STATES OF THE FEDERATION TO THE TCTI DEMONSTRATION SECONDARY SCHOOL, BIASE, CROSS RIVER STATE.

ATTACHMENTS

Please find attached the following:

  1. The Application Form for Admission into the TCTI Demonstration Secondary School, Biase, Cross River State
  2. The Advert for Admission into the TCTI Demonstration Secondary School, Biase, Cross River State
  3. Flier for Advertising the Admission

CHANNELS FOR ADVERTISING THE APPLICATION FOR ADMISSION

In communicating the advert for the admission, you are requested to reach out to parents, teachers, students and the general public using phone calls, SMS and various social media outlets such as WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, etc. The directories or lists of Cross River State indigenes living outside Cross River State, interested non-Cross River State indigenes and Parents in the Diaspora can be sought for from:

  1. Cross River Development and Cultural Associations, Town Unions and Social Clubs at the various State and Local Government Headquarters
  2. Churches and Mosques, Christian and Muslim Associations
  3. Higher Institutions- Universities, Polytechnics, Colleges of Education, etc.
  4. Military and paramilitary formations e.g. Army, Airforce, Navy, Police, Civil Defence, Federal Road Safety Corps, Customs, Immigration, etc.
  5. Associations such as Nigeria Union of Teachers, Nigeria Union of Journalists, Nigeria Medical Association, ASUU, etc.
  6. Ministries, Departments and Agencies e.g. TRCN, NTI, FME, etc.
  7. Federal Secretariat, Federal Government Colleges, etc.
  8. Market Associations
  9. Liaison Offices
  10. Banks and other institutions
  11. Judiciary, Legislature, and Executive arms of Government, etc.

SALE OF APPLICATION FORMS

Fee: The Admission Application Form is available online and is herewith attached. A non-refundable Application Fee of Five Thousand Naira (N5,000.00) is payable to:

Account Name: Teachers Continuous Training Institute (TCTI), Biase
Bank/Account Number: First Bank of Nigeria Account No. 2041499399

Mode of Payment: No cash payment. No POS payment. No ATM payment. All payments shall be by online bank transfer. The narration should indicate clearly the Name of the Student that is applying for admission and Class applied for ( e.g. JSS1, JSS2, SS1 or SS2). Evidence of payment must be presented at the Entrance Exam Venue before being allowed to sit for it.

Mode of Application

Applicants are to download the Admission Form, print it out and fill accordingly. Thereafter, they are to come with the completed Admission Form to the Examination Centre with evidence of payment. Note that only those who show evidence of paying the non-refundable fee will be allowed to take the Entrance Examination.

Intrested Parents in Diaspora

Parents in diaspora that are interested in the admission of their children/wards into the school should contact 09125408021 or 08060481393 via WhatsApp for special online entrance examination arrangement.

ENTRANCE EXAMINATION

Entrance examination for entry into Junior and Senior Secondary sections of the Demonstration Secondary School shall be held as follows:

Examination Date: Saturday, July 16th 2022

Time: 9:00am – 10:00am

Examination Venue:

  1. For interested applicants within the Cross River State the Examination shall be held at The TCTI Demonstration Secondary School, Biase.
  2. For the States of Nigeria outside the Cross River State, the venue shall be at the respective State Headquarters which shall be communicated to applicants with evidence of payment of the non-refundable application fee. Such applicants should contact the phone numbers below indicating their state of location, not later than a ten (10) working days to the examination date.

Items Required For the Examination: Students should come along with the following items for the examination

  • Evidence of Payment of Non-Refundable Application Fee of N5,000.00
  • Mathematical set
  • Calculator
  • 2 Passport Photographs
  • Proof of Identification from Previous School
