Science Laboratories

Chemistry Laboratory

Our chemistry laboratory has workbenches with sink, water faucet and reagent rack. These workbenches can also have base cabinets to store glassware, apparatus etc. Special cabinets with chemicals. Workbenches are fitted with necessary water and gas connections to conduct various experiments. Apart from these, Teacher's table, first aid kit, safety chart, and fire extinguisher are provided.

Physics Laboratory

Our physics laboratort include some more sophisticated equipment, such as voltammeter, electroscope, potentiometer, telescopes, microscopes, spectroscopes; electromagnets etc. These are used to conduct every type of physics lab experiments in school or university level

Biology Laboratory

Biology Laboratory has workbenches with sink, water faucet, and reagent rack.

Agricultural Laboratory

Built and equipped for research and Demonstration On Agriculture.
