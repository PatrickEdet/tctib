October 21st 2022

INVITATION TO MONITOR

The Teachers Continuous Training Institute, (TCTI), Biase is pleased to invite stakeholders and members of the public to monitor the BASELINE ASSESSMENT OF DIGITAL LITERACY PROFICIENCY SKILL LEVELS OF TEACHERS AND OTHER WORKFORCE IN CROSS RIVER STATE.

TCTI is conducting the first phase of Baseline Assessment of Digital literacy proficiency skill level of 1,000 (one thousand) persons in Cross River State among Primary, Secondary, Vocational and Technical Teachers, Youths, Public Service Workforce and in Informal Sector.

The purpose of the exercise is to establish digital literacy proficiency of the selected participants with a view to filling necessary gaps on Digital Literacy knowledge and skills needed for global employability and competitiveness.

The four day assessment exercise will commence at 10am daily at the following centers:

1. Monday, October 24 at the University of Cross River State, Calabar.

2. Tuesday Oct 25, at TCTI, Biase.

3. Wednesday Oct 26 at CR-Institute of Technology and Management (CR-ITM), Ugep.

4. Thursday Oct 27, at FCE, Obudu.

Teachers Continuous Training Institute, Biase, Cross River State is the first Nigerian Public Institution to be licensed as Certiport Authorized Testing Centre for Digital literacy testing and training to global certifications in over 1,000 skills.

After the baseline assessment exercise, the institute shall undertake the training of some of the participants in Digital Literacy proficiency skill, based on the IC3 Digital Literacy standard.

`

Amas Peter Maka. (Information Officer, TCTI, Biase, Cross River State)
