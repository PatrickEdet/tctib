21st CENTURY PIONEER TEACHERS OF TCTI DEMONSTRATION SCHOOL BIASE

Twenty three (23) Teaching Staff of Demonstration Secondary School, TCTI Biase were employed in December, 2021, out of the 901 applicants after going through the Institute's competitive subject-based tests and an interactive session with the TCTI Management.

The Demonstration School, which has state of the art teaching and learning facilities serves as a practical laboratory for in-service teachers who engage in retraining workshops in the Institute.
